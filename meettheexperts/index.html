Prof Peter Schellinger

Peter Schellinger is Chairman of the Departments of Neurology and Neurogeriatrics at the Clinical Centre Johannes Wesling in Minden, Germany

Prof Professor Schellingerâ€™s main research interests are the diagnosis and therapy of acute ischaemic stroke and intracranial haemorrhage, secondary prophylaxis and neuroprotection, and neurological critical care. He has contributed to numerous major acute stroke and intracranial haemorrhage trials and secondary prophylaxis trials for stroke since 1994.

He is also a board member of various International and German neurological societies / organisations such as WSO, ESO, ASA, DGN and DSG, and member of the Executive Committee, German Society of Neurocritical Care and Emergency Medicine (DGNI). He is also a fellow member of the American Heart Organization. He was member of the editorial boards / committees of European Neurology (2006-2010) and Stroke (2007-2013) and review board member of Lancet, Lancet Neurology, Stroke, Cerebrovascular Diseases and others.
