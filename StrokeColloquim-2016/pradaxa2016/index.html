Nickenig studied medicine at the Rheinische Friedrich-Wilhelms University of Bonn . In 1991 he was in Bonn with an examination of immunosuppression in cytostatic therapy to Dr. med. PhD . After working as a medical assistant at the Medical University Polyclinic in Bonn, he spent two years after Atlanta . After working at the Cardiology Department of the University Hospital in Cologne, he was from 2000 to 2005 Managing Senior Physician at the Department of Internal Medicine III (Cardiology) and lecturer at the University Hospital of the Saarland in Homburg .
